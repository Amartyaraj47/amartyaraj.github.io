01. About Me

Hey, I’m Amartyaraj Kumar— an electronics engineer, photonics enthusiast, and someone who’s trying to make photons do math for a living. I work at the cutting edge of photonic computing architecture, where light—not electrons—does the heavy lifting in computation.

I try to design and build scalable optical systems that use quantum properties of light to perform logic, carry information, and power the next generation of computing. From waveguides to entangled photons, I turn quantum weirdness into working tech.

In short: I bend light, wrestle with wavefunctions, and turn quantum headaches into hardware—and I love every entangled second of it.
